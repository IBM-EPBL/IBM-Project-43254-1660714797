CRUDE OIL PRICE PREDICTOR


Crude oil price fluctuations have a far reaching impact on global economies and thus price forecasting can assist in

minimising the risks associated with volatility in oil prices. Price forecasts are very important to various stakeholders:

governments, public and private enterprises, policymakers, and investors.This application aims to help predict crude oil price

with the help of Time series forecasting , by using the data of crude oil prices over the past 10 days.
